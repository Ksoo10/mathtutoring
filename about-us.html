How It Works

I know that reaching out for help can be intimidating. That's why I make it as easy as possible to get started. Just follow these simple steps and we'll be on our way to helping your child succeed in math.

Step 01

Send me a message through the form below. I will review what you share and get back to you within 24 hours to set up a free 15 minute discovery call.

Step 02

On the discovery call we go over your child's specific challenges, their grade level, and what they need most right now.

Step 03

Based on our call I put together a session plan built specifically around your child's curriculum and learning pace so every lesson moves them forward.

Step 4

We schedule the first session and begin. No long commitments. No contracts. Just a clear next step toward your child feeling capable and confident in math.
