Meet Mr.Mcdonald

Adrian holds a Bachelor's Degree from Delaware State University and has spent the past 5 Years teaching students in 3rd and 6th grade. He understands exactly where young learners get stuck from multiplication and division in 3rd grade to ratios and pre algebra in 6th grade. Every concept is covered with patience and clarity.

Throughout his 5 Years in the classroom, Adrian has seen firsthand what makes math click for young students. He builds every session around your child's current schoolwork so nothing feels disconnected or confusing. Whether your child is mastering multiplication tables in 3rd grade or navigating ratios and pre algebra in 6th grade, Adrian meets them with a clear plan and a calm, encouraging approach.

Your Questions About Adrian Answered

What grades does Adrian tutor?

We work specifically with students in 3rd grade and 6th grade. Every session is built around the exact curriculum your child is following in school.

My child is really struggling with math. Can he help?

Absolutely. He specializes in students who feel left behind or frustrated by math. Sessions start from the foundation and build up at a pace that works for your child.

Where is he located? Can we meet in person?

Adrian is based in Dover, Delaware and welcomes in person sessions at his location. Families outside Dover can also connect through online sessions using a kid friendly platform with a shared digital whiteboard.

How will I know how my child is progressing?

After each session a quick update is shared covering what was taught, where your child improved, and what to focus on next so you are never left wondering how things are going.
