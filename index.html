What grades does Adrian tutor?

I work exclusively with 3rd and 6th graders and after 5 years in the classroom I know their curriculum inside and out. Every session is built around exactly what your child is learning right now in school.

In a classroom your child is one of many. Here every session is built entirely around your child's pace, their gaps, and their specific grade level goals. Nothing gets skipped over or rushed.

Most students see strong results with one to two sessions per week. A plan is always discussed during the free discovery call based on your child's specific needs.

Adrian is based in Dover, Delaware and welcomes in person sessions at his location. Families outside Dover can also connect through online sessions using a kid friendly platform with a shared digital whiteboard.
